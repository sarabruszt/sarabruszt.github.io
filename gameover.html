Game Over

By Luna Miao & Sara Bruszt

Game Over is a sound remix, only found sounds were used.

IDEA

For the project Luna and I used video game sounds, video game trailer sounds and some other audio about war, like speech and sound of bombs. We found all our audios on Youtube. The project starts off with happy video game sounds like Pokemon Go or Packman but later on it turns very cruel until GAME OVER from Supermario!

The project is a commentary on violent video games.


Remix Edited in Reaper and Adobe Audition

Sound Design, Remix
