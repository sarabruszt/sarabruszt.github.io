Controls

Controls is a Unser Experience Design Project. Scroll Down!

IDEA

Controls is a mobile app designed for insulin pump users. Insulin pumps are a great way to control type 1 diabetes, however according to research users want a mobile app with a redesigned interface to control their pump.

USER STORY

Insulin pumps are usually kept under clothing and they are especially troublesome to use in a social situation. The user would have to take out the pump from under the clothes and this could be uncomfortable in front of friends or colleges, maybe on a date.

USER RESEARCH

The research was conducted on social media platforms. I observed that most respondents reported difficulties with hiding their insulin pumps under certain dresses and as well they have difficulties controlling them in those situations.

PERSONA

I created a persona according to the research findings.

FLOW CHART

I created a flow chart, remapped the most important functions of insulin pumps and imagined how it will look like in a form of a mobile app.
