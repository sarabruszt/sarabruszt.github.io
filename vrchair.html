VR Chair

The VR Chair is a special viewing experience of a painting in virtual space.

IDEA

This is an experimental piece. I noticed, when walking in a museum, one would sometimes not pay enough attention to a painting. I was wondering how the viewing of a painting in a virtual space would differ from viewing a physical painting.

DESIGN

The light balls in neon colors provide a night club like atmosphere. One can hear classical music in the background. By this I meant to create confusion and I wanted the user to immerse in the sound and visual experience while looking at the painting. The floor is made of water which gives a calm feeling. In contrast, the walls are reflective which adds to the modern, night club like feeling.

TECHNOLOGY

The project was built in Unreal Engine. For display I used HTC vive headset without controllers.
